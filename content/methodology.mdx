Our goal is to help you pick a workflow that is **repeatable** and **predictable**, not just “best sounding once”.

## What we check (typical checklist)

- **Voice quality**: naturalness, stability, pronunciation, artifacts
- **Captions accuracy**: punctuation, diarization, timestamps, error rate on real samples
- **Workflow fit**: batch processing, exports (SRT/VTT), editing steps, integrations
- **Pricing & limits**: character/minute limits, tiers, overages, commercial terms
- **Reliability**: latency, queueing, retries, known limitations

## Sources

We prioritize official sources (pricing pages, docs, release notes) and cross-check claims inside the product when possible.
