## FAQ

### How many words do I need for natural pacing?

For English and French we often target roughly **140–160 words/minute** with a **12–18%** pause ratio. The goal is consistency so captions stay aligned even if you trim filler later.

### Do you keep stems or just the final mix?

Keep stems until the avatar/video platform accepts a locked mix. It prevents lip‑sync drift when you re-export, and lets you drop music or SFX without regenerating the voice.

### What if I only have subtitles?

You can rebuild a voice track from SRT/VTT text, but add punctuation and pauses where needed so the generated voice matches your intended timing.
